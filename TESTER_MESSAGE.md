Send this to each tester individually. Personalize the opening line so it doesn't feel like a mass message.

You can send it as a text, a DM, or an email. Texts and DMs tend to land better.

## Quick Notes For Julianne

- Send to one person at a time, not a group chat. Group chats kill honesty.
- Don't watch the conversation in real time. Let them respond when they're ready.
- If they say "this is great!" and nothing else, gently ask: "Was there a moment that felt off, even a little?" Real feedback usually starts with the second question.
- Don't defend anything yet. Just listen, take notes, thank them. We'll fix things based on patterns.
- If someone has a hard reaction (something brings up a lot of feeling), check in on them as a person first, the app feedback can wait.
